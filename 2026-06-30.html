AI agents are not your “coworkers” ★★★☆☆
MIT Tech Review AI  ·  2026-06-29

Marketing AI agents as "coworkers" or "employees" can negatively impact human performance and accountability, according to a recent study. Researchers found that people caught 18% fewer errors when work was attributed to an "AI employee" rather than a chatbot. Despite this, major tech companies are increasingly promoting AI agents as digital colleagues, with many managers already adopting this framing.
