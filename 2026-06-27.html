Why everyone from OpenAI to SpaceX is building their own chips (and turning up the heat on Nvidia) ★★★★☆
TechCrunch AI  ·  2026-06-26

Companies including OpenAI, Google, Apple, and SpaceX are increasingly developing their own custom AI chips, such as OpenAI's 'Jalapeño' built with Broadcom. The move represents a significant shift in the AI chip market, challenging Nvidia's long-standing dominance.
