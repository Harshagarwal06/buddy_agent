Trump drops restrictions on Anthropic’s Mythos and Fable models ★★★★☆
TechCrunch AI  ·  2026-07-01

The U.S. government has lifted export restrictions on Anthropic's advanced AI models, Mythos and Fable, which had previously cut off public access. Anthropic will restore access to these models on July 1 after agreeing to proactively detect security risks, collaborate with the U.S. government on protocols, and report malicious activity. The initial ban, imposed on June 12, had made it impractical for Anthropic to offer the models to foreign nationals without special approval.

Anthropic's Claude Fable 5 is set to return online globally, starting Wednesday, after weeks of negotiations with the Trump administration.
